A regular member can do the following things (tenatively):

Keyboard related: 
- Create a new keyboard
- Edit parts of the keyboard
- Delete a keyboard from their portfolio.

Portfolio related:
- View their portfolio (list of all keyboards that they have built).
- Remove keyboard from portfolio.
- Modify their portfolio.

Workshop related:
- Join a workshop
- Host a workshop
- Modify a workshop (if they're the host).

Contest related:
- Join a contest

Posts related: [Hieu]
- Like a post
- Comment on a post
- Modify a post (if they're the poster).
- Make a post
